Kenyan Pilau

Fragrant spiced rice dish

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g beef, cubed
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp pilau masala
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Brown meat in oil and set aside
  2. Sauté onions until caramelized
  3. Add garlic and spices, cook for 1 minute
  4. Add rice and stir to coat with spices
  5. Add stock and meat, simmer until rice is cooked

Nutritional Information

Calories: 420 | Carbs: 65g | Protein: 25g | Fiber: 3g
