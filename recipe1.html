Ugali & Sukuma Wiki

Kenya's staple food combination

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 bunch sukuma wiki (collard greens)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Gradually add maize flour while stirring
  3. Cook for 10-15 minutes until firm
  4. For sukuma wiki: Sauté onions, add tomatoes, then greens
  5. Serve hot with meat or stew

Nutritional Information

Calories: 250 | Carbs: 55g | Protein: 5g | Fiber: 3g
